• Responsible for collecting data from community-dwelling older adults living in BC, Canada. Data was consisting of cognitive status, walking paradigm (gait) including all spatial and temporal gait parameters such as stride velocity, stride length/time/width, swing time, etc. Electronic walkway system, GAITRite, also Vicon Motion Capture system, and eight 9-axis IMUs were used to collect gait data.
  • In charge of data Processing, including data cleaning, negating IMU signal, filtering, resampling, cropping, and finding walking paradigm features by means of LabVIEW and Python programming.
  • Developing machine learning models to predict the likelihood of future falls in older adults in order to provide special intervening techniqus and treatments.
